Gnomeo & Juliet

An epic tale on a tiny scale.

Gnomeo and Juliet is a fun, new take on Romeo and Juliet, but with garden gnomes. Two neighboring gardens are always fighting. Gnomeo and Juliet meet, fall for each other, and try to keep their love a secret. Things get pretty crazy between the two sides, but in the end, Gnomeo and Juliet help everyone realize that love is more important than old grudges, and the gardens finally make peace.
