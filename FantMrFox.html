Fantastic Mr Fox

"I don't want to live in a hole anymore." - Mr. Fox

Fantastic Mr. Fox is a 2009 stopmotion animated movie directed by Wes Anderson, It is based off of the children's book by Roald Dahl of the same name. The film is known for its unique visual style that is prominent in a lot Wes Anderson's work, creating a memorable experience for the viewer. the story follows Mr. Fox, trying to create a better life for his family but struggling to stop his old habits of stealing from the local farmers. ultimately leading to putting his family in danger.

Cast

    George Clooney as Mr. Fox

    Meryl Streep as Mrs. Fox

    Jason Schwartzman as Ash Fox

    Wallace Wolodarsky as Kylie
