Get Out

Get Out (2017) is a groundbreaking psychological horror-thriller directed by Jordan Peele. The film follows Chris, a Black man who visits his white girlfriend’s family estate, only to uncover a terrifying secret lurking beneath their seemingly welcoming facade. Blending horror with sharp social commentary, Get Out explores themes of racism, manipulation, and identity, creating an unsettling atmosphere that lingers long after the credits roll.
