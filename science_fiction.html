Science Fiction

To boldy go where no man has gone before. Captain James Tiberius Kirk

introduction

Science fiction to me is the a philosophy of writing which asks could this be plausible given a indeterminate period of time of human advancement and change given these circumstances, with a heavy caveat of the rule of cool applied to it. Meaning that as long as something is interesting and or appealing enough in media we are willing so suspend our disbelief because believing is more fun. As science fiction is the abstract grouping of lots of works of media in many forms be they written or film based, there is many definitions that can be applied and it is less about which one is correct or which film is truly a sci-fi movie as most movies encompass multiple genres anyway. It is more about does this film have the soul of sci-fi which to me has always been a genre that is captivating as it gets me to question my understandings and views.
