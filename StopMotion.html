Fantastic Mr Fox

Fantastic Mr. Fox is a 2009 American stop-motion animated comedy film directed by Wes Anderson, based on Roald Dahl's 1970 children's novel of the same name.

Isle of Dog

Isle of Dogs is a 2018 American stop-motion animated science fiction comedy-drama film directed by Wes Anderson.

Kubo and the Two Strings

Kubo and the Two Strings is a 2016 American stop-motion animated action fantasy film directed by Travis Knight.

Coraline

Coraline is a 2009 American stop-motion animated dark fantasy horror film directed by Henry Selick.
