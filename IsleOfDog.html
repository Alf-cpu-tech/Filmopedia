Isle of Dog

"Don't ask me to fetch that stick." - Chief

Isle of Dogs is a 2018 stop-motion animated film directed by Wes Anderson. the film takes place in a future where dogs have become over saturated in japan and are exiled to Trash Island. the story follows a boy called atari in search of his dog spots.

Cast

    Byran Cranston as Chief

    Meryl Streep as Atari

    Edward Norton as Rex

    Jeff Goldblum as Duke
